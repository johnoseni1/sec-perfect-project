THE STORY

We are dropping 10K NFTs that celebrate fashion, style, culture and community. We are partnering with You Gotta Believe, an organization that helps teenagers in foster care find permanent families in NYC.

This project was inspired by our mother and son team. Trey, age 22, has provided the inspiration and styles for these NFTs. Growing up in Brooklyn, he beat the odds by graduating from high school and starting his own career, when many people said that he couldn’t do it.

Youth don’t have it easy in urban areas like Brooklyn. With all the bullying, temptations, school violence and lack of male role models, many have seen things some people can't even imagine. This project will generate awareness to encourage youth to get involved in positive outlets such as art and technology, while at the same time supporting youth in foster care.‍

Designs

Our fleeboys have been inspired by styles typical in Brooklyn and other artistic urban areas. We paid special attention to the art and culture inspired by murals and other common city scenes. Clothing, haircuts, facial designs and accessories play a big role in the styles of these NFTs. Humans are common, but elves, genies and royalty are rare.

collecting

1. GET A METAMASK WALLET

Download the METAMASK.IO extension for the CHROME, FIREFOX, or BRAVE browser or METAMASK app on mobile. This will allow you to make purchases and collect NFTs on Ethereum and, once installed, can be found in the extensions tab.

If you are on mobile, you MUST use the Metamask app browser.

2. ACQUIRE ETHEREUM

We suggest that you do this in advance. Minting for 0N1 Force NFTs will be between .07 and .09 ETHEREUM per transaction (plus gas).

You are able to purchase ETHEREUM through the METAMASK wallet using Wyre or Send Ethereum from an exchange like Coinbase.

3. MINT AN 0N1 NFT

Once launched (early August), a dAPP to connect your wallet, select quantity, and purchase will appear.

First, you will connect your wallet and sign. Then, select the amount of 0N1 you wish to mint, and finally select MINT to make the transaction. You will pay the amount per 0N1 NFT plus gas (transaction fee).

You will be able to submit a transaction for 20 0N1 in a single tx.
